Identification of future developments opportunities for the project


We liked how the page turned out in the end, however, we identified some development opportunities we have to make it better, and more impactful with our target audience.
Some of them are:

  • Adding more material for each semester
  • Implementing a forum where students can share their doubts, tips, and material they have to help other students
  • Having a login, so users get their personalized content based on their profile and need
  • Improving the site's responsiveness so the tablet and smartphone version feel better to use
